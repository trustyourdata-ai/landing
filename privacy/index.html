Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 9, 2026

TrustYourData ("TrustYourData", "we", "us", or "our") provides a service for dataset readiness analysis, reporting, and related tooling through the TrustYourData website and related services (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website, join our waitlist, contact us, or use the Service.

By using the Service, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through:

  • the TrustYourData website;
  • waitlist, beta access, and contact forms;
  • communications with us by email or other supported channels;
  • use of the TrustYourData Service, including dataset analysis and report generation.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, services, or platforms that may be linked from our website or integrated with the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information.

2.1 Contact and account information

We may collect information such as:

  • name;
  • email address;
  • company name;
  • job title or role;
  • any information you provide when contacting us or joining the waitlist.

2.2 Service usage information

We may collect technical and operational information about how the Service is used, such as:

  • timestamps of requests;
  • analysis job status;
  • report generation events;
  • browser type, device type, operating system, and approximate technical environment;
  • IP address or network-derived information;
  • diagnostic logs, error reports, and performance metrics.

2.3 Dataset-related information

When you use the Service, we may process data and metadata associated with uploaded or analyzed datasets, including:

  • file format and file size;
  • schema information;
  • column names and inferred data types;
  • statistical summaries and quality signals;
  • readiness findings and generated reports;
  • configuration inputs, task intent, and selected analysis options.

Depending on the mode of operation, we may also temporarily process dataset contents to perform the requested analysis.

2.4 Website analytics and similar data

We may collect website usage information through privacy-focused analytics tools or infrastructure providers, such as page views, referring pages, browser details, and other aggregate traffic information.

2.5 Communications

If you contact us, we may keep records of your messages, support requests, and related correspondence.

3. How We Use Information

We may use information for the following purposes:

  • to operate, maintain, and provide the Service;
  • to generate dataset readiness reports, findings, and related artifacts;
  • to administer waitlists, beta access, and onboarding;
  • to respond to inquiries, support requests, and other communications;
  • to monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and improve reliability;
  • to detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, misuse, or security incidents;
  • to enforce our Terms of Service and other applicable policies;
  • to comply with legal obligations;
  • to improve the Service, provided that we do not use uploaded datasets for model training unless explicitly stated and separately agreed.

4. Dataset Handling

TrustYourData is designed to help users assess whether datasets are ready for AI, machine learning, and related analytical use cases.

4.1 Purpose limitation

Datasets submitted to the Service are processed solely for the purpose of performing the requested analysis, generating reports, and supporting related service functionality.

4.2 No training on user datasets

Unless explicitly stated otherwise in a separate written agreement, TrustYourData does not use uploaded user datasets to train foundation models, general-purpose machine learning models, or external commercial AI systems.

4.3 Modes of operation

Depending on the product configuration or feature selected, dataset handling may differ:

  • API or hosted mode: datasets or portions of datasets may be transmitted to and processed on secure infrastructure controlled by us or our service providers for the purpose of generating analysis results;
  • privacy-oriented or local mode: certain analysis steps may occur locally, and only limited metadata, summaries, or derived signals may be transmitted to our systems.

Not all modes may be available at all times.

4.4 User responsibility

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the necessary rights and lawful basis to submit data to the Service, including personal data, confidential information, or regulated data.

You should not submit data to the Service if you do not have authority to do so.

5. Data Ownership

Users retain ownership of their datasets and other content submitted to the Service.

This Privacy Policy does not transfer ownership of user data to TrustYourData.

We process user data only as necessary to provide the Service, comply with law, protect the Service, and enforce our rights.
